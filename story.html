Two Worlds,
One Vision

As a first-generation born and raised Ontarian, I've always felt the pull of two worlds — one rooted in the rich traditions of my family and the other shaped by the diverse, vibrant culture of my home.

Growing up in a diverse city, gatherings were often filled with stories, where wine was a symbol of celebration, connection, and tradition.

It was only natural that as I carved my own path, I found myself drawn to the idea of creating something that fused both my upbringing and the opportunity this beautiful country provides.

Meet the Founder

Jasneet Mander

“Every bottle is a bridge between where I come from and the land that raised me.”

Jasneet founded Mander Wines in 2024 with a singular vision: to craft wines that honour both heritage and terroir. With hands in the vineyard and eyes on every barrel, she is personally involved in each step — from handpicking the grapes at the Laundry Vineyard to overseeing the aging in French oak.

Mander Wines is not just a label; it is a personal journey made to share.
