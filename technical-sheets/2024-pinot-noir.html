Vinification & Aging

Handpicked on September 20, 2024, and gently fruit-sorted, this small-lot Pinot Noir showcases a fruit-forward profile. Aged 10 months in French oak, the wine reveals a harmonious integration of bright fruit and delicate oak complexity. Each barrel carefully monitored to preserve the vineyard's natural expression.

Founder's Note

“As a first-generation born and raised Ontarian, I've always felt the pull of two worlds. Wine was a symbol of celebration, connection, and tradition. Every bottle is a bridge between where I come from and the land that raised me.”

— Jasneet Mander, Founder & Vintner

Tasting Profile

Vibrant notes of cherry and strawberry, layered with subtle hints of soft spice. Medium-bodied with a silky texture, the wine is elegantly structured and lingers with a long finish.
