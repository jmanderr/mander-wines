2024 Pinot Noir

Laundry Vineyard • Lincoln Lakeshore VQA

This small-lot Pinot Noir showcases a fruit-forward profile with vibrant notes of cherry and strawberry, layered with subtle hints of soft spice. Medium-bodied with a silky texture, the wine is elegantly structured and lingers with a long finish. Aged 10 months in French oak, the wine reveals a harmonious integration of bright fruit and delicate oak complexity.
